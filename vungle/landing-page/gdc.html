China For the Win!
What Publishers Need to Know to Succeed in This Emerging Market

12:30 - 1:30pm @ West Hall Rm 2011

The mobile explosion is firmly underway in China, with nearly 550 million active smartphone and tablet devices in 2014 alone--most with a mobile game installed.

Learn tangible tips and insight for developing in China - from localizing your game to navigating app stores and monetization for this massive new market.

Presented By:

Colin Behr

VP of Business & International Relations

Colin built Vungle's publishing business from the beginning, and now leads strategy, partnerships and international expansion. He has been leading Vungle's efforts entering the Chinese market including opening up a new Vungle office in Beijing.

Monetize, Don’t Compromise!
The Unbreakable Rules of User Experience in Ad Monetization

2:00 - 3:00pm @ West Hall Rm 2011

What if your users actually wanted to see ads? Drive substantial revenue by offering users smooth and engaging opt-in advertising that never disrupts the game flow. This session covers bottom-up planning and design to give your users a high-end ad experience, while opt-in controls ensure rapid monetization and increased engagement.

Presented By:

Michal Pilawski

5:00pm - 6:00pm @ West Hall Rm 2011

Learn how Tango makes money and acquires new users. Tango’s VP of Game Publishing, Jim Ying & Vungle’s VP of Sales will discuss everything from monetization best practices to Tango’s global gaming platform.

Ben Bear

Featuring:

Jim Ying

VP of Game Publishing @ Tango

Jim Ying is Vice President of Game Publishing at Tango, where his team signs and publishes games through the Tango platform.
